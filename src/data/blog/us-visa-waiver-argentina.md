In July 2025, Argentina submitted an application to rejoin the **US Visa Waiver Program (VWP)**. If approved, Argentine passport holders could once again travel to the United States for tourism or business without obtaining a visa in advance.

## Why this matters for CBI

Citizenship by Investment programs are often evaluated in part by the strength of the passport they confer. Visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to the US, the UK, and the Schengen area is a major draw. Argentina's re-entry into the VWP would significantly increase the value of Argentine citizenship for many investors.

## Timeline and next steps

The US Department of Homeland Security evaluates VWP applications based on security, reciprocity, and overstay rates. There is no fixed timeline for a decision. For now, the application is under review—and for those considering the Argentina CBI program, it is a factor to watch as the program develops.
